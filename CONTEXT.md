# EduTools domain language

## Primary categories

- **Interactive learning object** — a focused experience a learner can manipulate, question, practise with or explore.
- **Content distribution** — a guide, reader, presentation, course or publication system that turns source material into a usable experience.
- **Agent skill** — a reusable instruction package through which an agent performs a bounded educational, research or management task.
- **Desktop application** — maintained local software shaped around a complete working practice.

## Relationships with AI

- **Built by AI** — AI assisted in making the software or content.
- **Powered or augmented by AI** — a model is called while a person uses the experience.
- **Delegated to AI** — an agent carries out bounded work through a skill or embedded workflow.

The three relationships overlap. They are not maturity levels.

## Other terms

- **Reusable pattern** — a secondary cross-category collection for portable structures such as Single HTML Document and Online Reader Framework.
- **First edition** — included in the initial 2026 public catalogue.
- **Later** — retained for future editorial consideration.
- **Undecided** — unresolved; neither public nor excluded.
- **Archive** — the preserved 2025 site, available for historical reference but not presented as current guidance.
