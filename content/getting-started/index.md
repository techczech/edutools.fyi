You do not need to begin by choosing a technology stack. Begin with the educational job and the form it needs.

Google AI Studio offers the quickest path to a visible interactive prototype. Codex becomes useful when the prototype needs to become a real project with local files, version history, reusable skills and deployment. Desktop applications make sense when the work depends on local archives, operating-system capabilities or a sustained personal workflow.
